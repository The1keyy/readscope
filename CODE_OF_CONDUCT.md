# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ReadScope is an open and welcoming project. Everyone who participates — whether contributing code, opening issues, giving feedback, or joining discussions — deserves to be treated with respect. We are committed to making this a space where people of all backgrounds and experience levels feel comfortable contributing.

---

## Our Standards

**We expect everyone to:**

- Be respectful and considerate in all communications
- Give and receive constructive feedback graciously
- Acknowledge and learn from mistakes without defensiveness
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Show patience with newcomers and those still learning

**We do not tolerate:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or behavior related to age, race, gender, sexuality, religion, disability, or background
- Dismissing or belittling someone's contributions or questions
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages, and any other forum where community members interact on behalf of ReadScope.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by opening a private issue or reaching out directly to the project maintainer. All reports will be handled with discretion and taken seriously.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of anyone who reports an incident.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Responses to violations may include:

- A private conversation and warning
- Temporary restriction from project participation
- Permanent removal from the project community

The response will be proportional to the nature and severity of the violation.
